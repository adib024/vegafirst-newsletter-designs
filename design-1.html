Cover Story

India's Plant Protein Revolution Is Here

By Palak Mehta · 5 min read · Photography by Ankit Sharma
Image Placeholder

The landscape of Indian cuisine is undergoing a seismic transformation. From bustling street-food stalls in Delhi to upscale restaurants in Bengaluru, plant-based protein alternatives are no longer niche—they're becoming mainstream. Industry reports suggest the Indian vegan food market will reach ₹8,500 crore by 2028, driven by health-conscious millennials and growing environmental awareness. This month, we explore the pioneers, the products, and the profound cultural shift making India one of the world's most exciting vegan frontiers.
