India's Plant Protein Revolution Is Here

By Palak Mehta · 5 min read · Photography by Ankit Sharma

The landscape of Indian cuisine is undergoing a beautiful transformation. From bustling street-food stalls in Delhi to upscale restaurants in Bengaluru, plant-based protein alternatives are becoming mainstream. The Indian vegan food market is projected to reach ₹8,500 crore by 2028, driven by health-conscious millennials and environmental awareness.
