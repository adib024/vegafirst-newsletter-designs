Cover Story

India's Plant Protein Revolution

Words by Palak Mehta · Photography by Ankit Sharma

The landscape of Indian cuisine is undergoing a remarkable transformation. From bustling street-food stalls in Old Delhi to Bengaluru's upscale dining rooms, plant-based protein alternatives are becoming mainstream. With the market projected to reach ₹8,500 crore by 2028, India stands at the forefront of a global movement toward compassionate, sustainable food systems.
