Lead Story
UK Supreme Court bans ‘milk’ labelling for dairy-free alternatives
By Priya Menon  •  London  •  March 12, 2026
The landmark ruling, delivered in a 4-1 decision, upholds an earlier ban on brands including Oatly from using dairy terminology in marketing materials distributed across the United Kingdom. Industry analysts say the decision could trigger copycat legislation in the EU and signal challenges ahead for India’s rapidly growing dairy-alternative beverage sector.
