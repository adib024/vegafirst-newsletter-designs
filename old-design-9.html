India's Plant Protein Revolution

How a new generation is transforming the subcontinent's relationship with food
BY PALAK MEHTA · PHOTOGRAPHY BY ANKIT SHARMA · 5 MIN READ

The landscape of Indian cuisine is undergoing a seismic transformation. From bustling street-food stalls in Delhi to upscale restaurants in Bengaluru, plant-based protein alternatives are no longer niche—they're becoming mainstream. Industry reports suggest the Indian vegan food market will reach ₹8,500 crore by 2028, driven by health-conscious millennials and growing environmental awareness. We explore the pioneers, products, and the cultural shift making India one of the world's most exciting vegan frontiers.
