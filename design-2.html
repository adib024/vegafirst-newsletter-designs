Editor’s Letter
A pivotal moment for plant-based India
When Marico invested ₹226 crore in a single plant-based brand, it sent a signal louder than any campaign ever could. We are no longer a niche. We are a movement with institutional backing, cultural momentum, and a generation of consumers who choose differently. This is the issue where we document that turning point — in business, in kitchens, in wardrobes, in hospital wards.
